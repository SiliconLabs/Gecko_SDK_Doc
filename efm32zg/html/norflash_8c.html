norflash.c File Reference

Implements the NORFLASH driver for Spansion S29GL128P90FFIR13. Spansion S29GL128P90FFIR13 is a 16MByte device organized in 128 sectors of 128KBytes each. The module can easily be tailored to suit other NOR flash devices. More...
