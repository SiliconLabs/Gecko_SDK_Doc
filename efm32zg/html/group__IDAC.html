IDAC
EMLIB

Detailed Description

Current Digital to Analog Conversion (IDAC) Peripheral API.

This module contains functions to control the IDAC peripheral of Silicon Labs 32-bit MCUs and SoCs. The IDAC can sink or source a configurable constant current, to bias internal circuits, or together with the ADC measure capacitance by injecting a controlled current into a component.
